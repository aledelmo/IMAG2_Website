The IMAG2 App

Clinical Anatomy Exploration

Powerful, user-friendly and complete. The IMAG2 App allows surgeons to bring patient specific models from the staff meetings directly to the OR. With everything in one place you can explore reconstructions, exams and diagnosis. Connect your laptop to the surgical robot to use 3D models as guidance, without having to get your head off the console.

Advanced Visualization and Interactivity

Check anatomical rapports between organs, vessels, nerves and tumors. Act independently on each structure to achieve fine visualization of regions of interest. Highlight and position models in the same space of the patient to choose the next surgical step more informed than ever!
