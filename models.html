Organs

The age of anatomy books is over. The time of 3D models has come.

Recent technology advancements were rarely followed by the adoption of disruptive applications by the medical community. Surgeons are still forced to prepare interventions navigating through 2D slices of noisy, gray level images. Until now!

Thanks to our expert clinicians and AI methods we are able to produce the most accurate 3D models on the market, prepared by surgeons for surgeons.

Kidneys, vessels, soft tissues, tumors and more. Let the 3D revolution help you!

Nerves

The added value of a precise representation of the peripheral nervous system makes the IMAG2 models a perfect companion for difficult surgeries, where every decisions focus on the preservation of as many functionality as possible.

The nerves anatomy, validated by the Lighsheet Project, allows to appreciate all the main abdominal plexuses as well as the degenerations introduced by tumoral masses or malformations.
